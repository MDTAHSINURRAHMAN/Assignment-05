Important FAQ

Where will my donation go?

Your donation will be directed towards providing essential supplies and aid to those affected by disasters and social movements in Bangladesh. We ensure that all contributions are used efficiently and transparently for relief efforts.

Can I donate anonymously?

Currently, our donation system does not support anonymous donations. However, we value your privacy, and all personal information provided during the donation process is kept confidential.

How can I support the cause without donating money?

While donations are the most direct way to help, you can also support our cause by spreading the word. Share our website and mission on social media or with friends and family to raise awareness about the issues we're addressing.

How will my donation help those in need?

Your donation will be used to deliver essential aid, including food, shelter, and medical supplies, to vulnerable communities in Bangladesh affected by emergencies. We focus on ensuring that every dollar is spent effectively to provide timely and meaningful support.
